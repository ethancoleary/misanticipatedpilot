In this study you will play one round of a hiring game.
You will make one decision which will earn you tokens (1 token = $0.01). You will receive a bonus payment equal to the cash value of your earnings from this decision.

In the game:

  1. You will be matched with 15 other Prolific users to form a society i.e. each society contains 16 participants. The users in your society also completed this experiment but at different times. Your decisions and theirs will determine your bonus payment.
  2. You will be assigned to a group labelled as Green or Purple.
  3. You will be assigned a productivity value.
  4. Your decision is to decide whether to apply for a job.
  5. An employer will choose which applicants to hire.
